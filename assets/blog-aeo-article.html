What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your content so that AI tools and answer engines can read, understand, and cite it accurately. Where traditional SEO targets ranking positions in a list of links, AEO targets the answer itself — the paragraph, the summary, the recommendation an AI generates in response to a natural-language question.

The goal isn't to get someone to click your link. It's to be the source the AI cites when someone asks about your category. — The AEO Principle

When people ask "what is AEO," the short answer is: it's the discipline of making your content the answer. In practice, AEO encompasses:

  • Structuring content in clear, answer-formatted language
  • Using FAQ schema and structured data markup
  • Building topical authority across related content
  • Providing clean, machine-readable versions of your content (including an llms.txt file)
  • Maintaining E-E-A-T signals (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust)

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?

Answer Engine Optimization is the practice of structuring content so AI tools — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Bing Copilot — can read, understand, and cite it accurately. Where SEO targets a ranking position, AEO targets the answer itself.

AEO vs SEO: what's the difference?

SEO optimizes for a position in a list of blue links. AEO optimizes to be the source an AI cites when generating a direct answer. SEO wins clicks; AEO wins citations. Most modern content strategies need both — and they share infrastructure inside HubSpot.

Which HubSpot tools support AEO?

Five HubSpot features map directly to AEO: SEO Topic Clusters for topical authority, Breeze AI for content generation, the AI Search Grader for measuring AI visibility, Content Hub for structured publishing and schema, and Smart CTAs for converting AI-referred traffic.

What is an llms.txt file?

llms.txt is an emerging standard — similar to robots.txt — that provides a clean, structured summary of a site for AI crawlers to read directly. It helps answer engines understand your business at a glance and is quickly becoming a baseline AEO signal.
