# HubSpot vs. Salesforce — Comparison Guide | Market Disrupt

A practical comparison of HubSpot and Salesforce for growing sales and revenue teams.

**HubSpot Strengths:**
- Faster to implement — weeks, not months
- Easier for non-technical admins to manage
- All Hubs (Sales, Marketing, Service) in one platform
- Lower total cost of ownership for mid-market
- Better out-of-the-box reporting

**Salesforce Strengths:**
- More customizable for complex enterprise requirements
- Larger ecosystem and AppExchange
- Better for highly regulated industries or deeply custom data models

**Who Should Choose HubSpot:**
Mid-market companies (50–500 employees) that need a CRM their team will actually use, with marketing and sales tools in one place, without a dedicated Salesforce admin.

**Who Should Choose Salesforce:**
Large enterprises with complex, custom requirements and dedicated technical resources to manage and maintain the platform.
