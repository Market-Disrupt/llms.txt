# Zendesk vs. Freshdesk — Comparison Guide | Market Disrupt

A practical comparison of Zendesk and Freshdesk for teams evaluating customer support platforms.

**Zendesk Strengths:**
- More powerful automation and workflow engine
- Better enterprise scalability and reporting
- Stronger AI and bot capabilities (AI Agents, Copilot)
- Larger ecosystem of integrations
- WFM and QA products built in

**Freshdesk Strengths:**
- Lower starting price for small teams
- Simpler setup for basic use cases
- Adequate for teams with straightforward support needs

**Who Should Choose Zendesk:**
Mid-market and enterprise teams that need real automation, SLA management, omnichannel support, and reporting they can trust. Teams planning to grow their support operation.

**Who Should Choose Freshdesk:**
Small teams (under 10 agents) with simple ticketing needs and a tight budget who don't need advanced automation.
