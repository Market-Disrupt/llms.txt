# HubSpot vs. Pipedrive — Comparison Guide | Market Disrupt

A practical comparison of HubSpot and Pipedrive for sales teams evaluating CRM options.

**HubSpot Strengths:**
- Full platform: CRM, Marketing Hub, Service Hub, and more in one tool
- Better automation capabilities across the full funnel
- Stronger reporting and revenue attribution
- Grows with the business — no need to switch CRMs later
- Free tier available

**Pipedrive Strengths:**
- Very simple pipeline-focused interface
- Low cost for small, purely sales-focused teams
- Quick to set up for basic pipeline tracking

**Who Should Choose HubSpot:**
Teams that need more than just pipeline tracking — email marketing, lead nurturing, service ticketing, or cross-team reporting. Anyone planning to scale.

**Who Should Choose Pipedrive:**
Small sales teams (under 10 reps) that only need pipeline management and don't require marketing or service tools.
