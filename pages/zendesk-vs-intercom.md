# Zendesk vs. Intercom — Comparison Guide | Market Disrupt

A practical comparison of Zendesk and Intercom for growing support and success teams.

**Zendesk Strengths:**
- Purpose-built ticketing and SLA management
- Better for high-volume support operations
- Stronger reporting and analytics
- More mature AI and automation
- WFM, QA, and workforce tools available

**Intercom Strengths:**
- Strong in-app messaging and product-led onboarding
- Better for low-volume, high-touch customer success
- Chat-first experience well-suited to SaaS onboarding

**Who Should Choose Zendesk:**
Teams handling significant ticket volume across email, chat, and phone. Support operations that need SLA management, agent productivity tools, and reliable reporting.

**Who Should Choose Intercom:**
SaaS companies primarily focused on in-app messaging, onboarding flows, and proactive outreach to active users — not high-volume reactive support.

**Market Disrupt Recommendation:** For support-focused teams, Zendesk is typically the better choice. Market Disrupt helps teams migrate from Intercom and implement Zendesk for scalable support.
