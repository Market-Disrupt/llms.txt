# Market Disrupt — Frequently Asked Questions

**What does Market Disrupt do?**
Market Disrupt implements, configures, and optimizes Zendesk, HubSpot, and Webflow for mid-market and enterprise businesses. Services include new setups, migrations, automation, integrations, and ongoing support.

**What platforms do you specialize in?**
Zendesk (Premier Partner), HubSpot (Platinum Solutions Partner), and Webflow (Certified Partner).

**Do you sell software licenses?**
Yes. Market Disrupt is a licensed Zendesk reseller. Clients buy licenses at the same price as going direct to Zendesk, and get expert implementation included.

**What size companies do you work with?**
Typically 50–500 employees, though we serve both SMBs and enterprise. We work with VP/Director-level buyers in CX, IT, Sales Ops, and RevOps.

**How do engagements work?**
Engagements start with discovery, move into implementation using a phased structure, and include optimization and support. Every engagement is senior-led.

**What industries do you serve?**
Technology/SaaS, fitness, healthcare, legal, automotive, insurance, telecom, and professional services.
